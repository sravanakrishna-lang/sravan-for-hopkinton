About the Candidate

27 Years of Leadership.

Sravana Krishna brings a rare combination to the Hopkinton School Committee race: deep technical expertise, proven community advocacy, and a personal stake in Hopkinton's schools as a parent.

The Platform

Four Pillars for Hopkinton Schools

A concrete, funded vision for Hopkinton's students — from the classroom to careers, from the mainstream to every learner who needs support.

01

General Education Academies

Sravan will champion the proven Academy model — AI, Medical, and Business pathways that have delivered results in districts like ours. The goal: bring that same college and career preparation to Hopkinton students.

Community-driven and parent-led — these Academies succeed with the buy-in of families and educators who shape them together.

AI · Medical · Business
02

Strengths-Based Special Education

Connect every special needs student to the same Academy opportunities through strengths-based pathway assessments. Hopkinton's special education students have talents and ambitions — the School Committee's job is to unlock them.

SEPAC · IEP · Inclusion
03

Real Equity — No Dead Ends

Students who miss competitive program cuts don't disappear. They get a personalized support roadmap to close gaps and try again. Public school is not a marketplace — every child who doesn't make a cut deserves a clear path forward, not an open door to nowhere.

Access · Support · Fairness
04

AI & Innovation for Hopkinton

Sravan will translate proven AI and process optimization case studies into practical solutions for Hopkinton — not as mandates, but through genuine collaboration and community buy-in. Smart tools, thoughtfully adopted.

AI · Innovation · Collaboration

Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions, Answered

When is the Hopkinton School Committee election?

The Hopkinton Annual Town Election is on Monday, May 18, 2026. Polls are open from 7 AM to 8 PM. All registered Hopkinton voters are eligible to vote in the School Committee race.

What is Sravana Krishna's background?

Sravan is a senior technology executive with 27 years of experience and a Master's in Computer Science with an AI and machine learning specialization. He serves on four Hopkinton town advisory boards and serves as Treasurer of Friends of SEPAC.

What are the Hopkinton School Committee Academies Sravan is proposing?

Sravan will champion the proven Academy model for Hopkinton schools — AI, Medical, and Business pathways an AI Academy with industry certifications, a Medical Academy modeled after nationally recognized programs, and a Business Academy preparing students for entrepreneurship and finance careers.

What is Sravan's position on special education in Hopkinton?

Sravan believes every special needs student has identifiable strengths that connect to real career pathways. His platform calls for strengths-based pathway assessments that link every IEP student to the same Academy opportunities available to general education students.
